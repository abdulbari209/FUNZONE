Important Information

Free tickets, voucher tickets and tickets from previous seasons
  • Tickets without a specific date must be dated before visiting at fun zone website.
  • When dating your tickets, you may be charged a surcharge if the valid admission price at that time is higher than the purchase price of the tickets.
  • If the tickets are not dated at admissions check, entry to FunZone-Park cannot be guaranteed. A service fee of €10 per ticket will be charged for subsequent on-site dating.
  • Please note the expiry date of tickets and vouchers.
Limitation of FunZone-Park tickets and vouchers

The Pakistan regular limitation period applies: undated tickets and voucher tickets are valid for a further three full years from the date of purchase, until the end of the year. Free tickets can only be used during the specified season. After the specified season has ended, the free tickets are no longer valid and cannot be used.

Evening ticket during the Summer & Winter season

There is no surcharge when purchasing the evening ticket at the Europa-Park box office. The evening ticket is available from 04.11.2024 to 12.01.2025 (except 24/25.12.2024) and allows entry to FunZone-Park from 4pm.

Offer period 2024/2025:

  • 04.11.2024 – 20.12.2024: €37.00
  • 21.12.2024 – 06.01.2025: €42.00
  • 07.01.2025 – 12.01.2025: €37.00

Free entry for birthday children

Up to and including on their 12th birthday, children celebrating their birthday on the day of visit receive free entry to FunZone-Park when they present a valid ID!
